About The Event

TechFiesta is our annual technical symposium that brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts for a celebration of innovation and learning.

Where Technology Meets Innovation

TechFiesta 2024 is a three-day technical symposium organized by the Computer Science Department of ABC College. This year's theme is "Sustainable Technology for a Better Tomorrow".

The event will feature a series of workshops, technical competitions, hackathons, and keynote speeches from industry leaders and academic experts.

With over 50 events spread across multiple tracks, TechFiesta offers something for everyone - from beginners to advanced tech enthusiasts.
